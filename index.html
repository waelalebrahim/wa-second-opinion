Second Opinion

a VeriTrace tool

Most tools blend several AI answers into one confident reply — and hide where the models actually disagreed. Second Opinion does the opposite: it shows you what they agree on, what's contested, and what none of them can back up — then checks the contested parts against your source.

Free and open source (MIT). Use it, fork it, make it better — it's yours. Best of luck. — Wael
